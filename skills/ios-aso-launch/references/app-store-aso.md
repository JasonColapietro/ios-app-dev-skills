# App Store ASO Rules

## Keyword Selection

Choose a keyword when:

- users plausibly search it,
- competitors exist but are not all dominant incumbents,
- recent apps show the niche is alive,
- the app can satisfy the searcher's job directly,
- the term is not a competitor trademark.

## Metadata Rules

- Do not repeat words across name, subtitle, and keyword field.
- Do not include spaces after commas in the keyword field.
- Prefer one of singular/plural, not both.
- Front-load the highest-value keywords.
- Keep first two description lines plain and searchable.
- Use promo text for a current hook; it is not indexed and can change without a
  new binary.

## Screenshot Rules

- First screenshot: outcome.
- Second screenshot: differentiator.
- Avoid empty input boxes as the first image.
- Captions should use short user-language, not feature jargon.
- Export required device sizes from the source layout, not manual resizing.

## Measurement Loop

After launch, measure:

- keyword ranking,
- impressions,
- product page conversion,
- screenshot conversion if experiments are available,
- ratings count and review quality.

Then reorder keywords by live performance and rotate promo text/captions.
